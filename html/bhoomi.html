About Bhoomi

Land title ownership transfer is slow and tedious process rife in corruption and fraud. Bhoo.me utilizes blockchain to verify land ownership in registry process.

Blockchain is an incorruptible distributed digital ledger of economic transactions that can be programmed to record not just financial transactions but everything of value like land title records. Bhoo.me will make digital land transfer process more robust and rigid. I was an Web developer intern for Rumsan's Bhoomi Project. You can see my work by clicking below.
